Audio Feature Mixer
?
Audio Feature Weights Adjust how much each audio characteristic matters when matching tracks. 0 = ignore, 0.5 = low, 1.0 = normal, 1.5 = high priority.

Energy: Intensity and activity level (loud, fast, noisy = high).
Valence: Musical positiveness (happy, cheerful = high; sad, angry = low).
Danceability: Rhythm stability, beat strength, groove.
Acousticness: Confidence the track is acoustic (unplugged).
Tempo: BPM matching importance.
Loudness: Overall volume level.
Speechiness: Presence of spoken words.
Instrumentalness: Likelihood of no vocals.
Liveness: Presence of live audience.

ðŸ’¡ Boost Energy+Valence for workout playlists. Boost Acousticness for unplugged vibes.
